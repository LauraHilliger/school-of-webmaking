Exploring

In this module, you'll be exploring the pedagogies and theoretical frameworks behind Webmaker. At the same time, you'll learn to understand the Web as an ecosystem and will begin to see why an Open Web is so important.

fork

Building

The building module is all about building open educational resources that embed web literacy and making skills with other topics. We'll use open practices to make learning materials that are relevant, free and designed for remixing.

fork

Facilitating

Understanding the pedagogies and methods behind Webmaker is one thing, using these practices in the classroom or at a workshop is another. This module will help you run a room using open practices and participatory learning techniques.

fork

Connecting

In this module you will learn how to connect in your local community as well as with the global community of Webmaker. You will learn how amplifying your work will better your practice and the practice of others.
